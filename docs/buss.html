buss

Real-time bus tracking for your daily commute. Never miss your bus or your stop again.

You're standing at the bus stop, no idea if the bus is two minutes away or twenty. You check the timetable but it hasn't been accurate in months. buss fixes this.

It's a real-time bus tracker for the UK that shows you exactly where your bus is, predicts when it'll arrive, and tells you when to get off.

Track Your Bus in Real Time

See your bus moving on the map as it makes its way to your stop. Not a rough estimate or a countdown timer, but the actual bus, animated smoothly along the road in real time.

Never Miss Your Stop

buss knows when you're on the bus and when your stop is coming up. It sends you a notification to get ready so you're never caught off guard. If you somehow pass your stop, it'll let you know that too.

Live on Your Lock Screen

Once you're tracking a bus, your journey appears as a Live Activity on your lock screen and in the Dynamic Island. See delay status, arrival estimates, and your next stop at a glance without opening the app.

Smart Arrival Predictions

buss doesn't just read the timetable back to you. It runs multiple prediction methods in parallel, including the bus's live position and actual road routing, to give you an arrival estimate you can trust.

Save Your Commute

Add your regular bus routes and see the next departures at a glance. Pick a departure, tap track, and buss takes care of the rest. Each route gets its own colour-coded card so you can find it instantly.

Know When to Leave

When your bus is approaching, buss calculates how long it'll take you to walk to the stop. You'll know exactly when to head out the door so you arrive just in time.

No ads. No account required. Just open the app, save your route, and let buss handle the rest. Built for UK buses using official government data.

Download buss and take the guesswork out of your commute.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

Spade Studio Ltd ("we", "us", or "our") built buss as a real-time bus tracking app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use buss.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Saved Routes: Bus operator, line number, embark stop, and disembark stop for routes you save
  • Preferences: Notification settings, appearance preferences, and route colours

Information from Device Features

With your permission, buss may access:

  • Location (When In Use): To show nearby buses and bus stops on the map
  • Location (Always): To calculate walking time to your bus stop and notify you when to leave. You can use the app without granting this permission.
  • Notifications: To alert you when your bus is approaching or when to get off

You can deny or revoke these permissions at any time in Settings. The app remains functional with reduced features.

Automatically Collected Information

We do not automatically collect any personal information. buss does not include analytics, crash reporting, or telemetry of any kind.

How We Store Your Data

Local Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data is encrypted by iOS. Your saved routes, preferences, and cached timetable data never leave your device.

No Cloud Sync

buss does not sync data to iCloud or any other cloud service. Your data exists only on the device where you created it.

Network Requests

buss connects to external services solely to provide real-time bus information:

  • Bus Open Data Service (BODS): A UK government API operated by the Department for Transport, used to fetch real-time bus locations and timetable data
  • NaPTAN: A UK government database used to look up bus stop names and locations
  • Apple MapKit: Used to display maps and calculate walking routes

These requests contain no personal information. Location data is used on-device only and is not sent to any server.

Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services. buss does not collect telemetry or usage data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties. Your data stays on your device.

Data Retention

Local Data: Remains on your device until you delete it. You can clear cached timetable data at any time from Settings.

Saved Routes: Persist until you delete them within the app.

We (Spade Studio Ltd) do not have access to any of your data.

Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • View: All your data is visible within the app
  • Edit: You can modify saved routes and preferences at any time
  • Delete: Delete individual routes or clear all cached data in Settings
  • Permissions: Manage location and notification permissions via iOS Settings

Children's Privacy

buss does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences to track bus journeys.

Security

Your data is protected by:

  • iOS device encryption for local storage
  • Your device passcode and/or biometric authentication
  • No cloud storage or remote data transmission of personal data

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Apple App Store

buss is distributed through the Apple App Store and complies with Apple's App Store Guidelines and privacy requirements.

This privacy policy applies to the buss app available on the Apple App Store.

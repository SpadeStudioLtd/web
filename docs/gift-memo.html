Gift Memo

Capture gift ideas whenever inspiration strikes. Never forget the perfect gift again.

You spot the perfect gift for your mom in July, but her birthday isn't until December. By then, you've completely forgotten. Gift Memo fixes this.

It's a simple app for capturing gift ideas whenever inspiration strikes—so you're never scrambling at the last minute.

Never Lose a Gift Idea

Browsing Amazon or shopping online? Use the share button to save gift ideas directly to Gift Memo. No app switching, no trying to remember later.

Get Reminded Before It's Too Late

Set birthday reminders for the people you care about. Choose when to be notified: two weeks before, a few days before, or the day of. When the reminder arrives, your saved gift ideas are right there waiting.

Plan Together With a Partner

Share your gift list with your spouse or partner. Coordinate who's buying what, avoid duplicates, and make holiday planning easier.

Everything in One Place

  • Add people and their birthdays
  • Create custom events (anniversaries, holidays, etc.)
  • Save gift ideas with photos, prices, and links
  • Mark gifts as purchased
  • Set budgets per person or event

Syncs Across All Your Devices

Everything syncs automatically through iCloud. Save a gift idea on your iPhone, see it instantly on your iPad.

Who It's For

Parents tracking extended family birthdays. Partners coordinating holiday shopping. Anyone who spots great gifts months early but forgets them when the occasion arrives.

No complicated features. No social pressure. Just a simple tool to help you remember the people you care about—and the gifts they'd love.

Download Gift Memo and never forget the perfect gift again.

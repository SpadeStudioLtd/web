Gift Memo

Capture gift ideas whenever inspiration strikes. Never forget the perfect gift again.

You spot the perfect gift for your mom in July, but her birthday isn't until December. By then, you've completely forgotten. Gift Memo fixes this.

It's a simple app for capturing gift ideas whenever inspiration strikes—so you're never scrambling at the last minute.

Never Lose a Gift Idea

Browsing Amazon or shopping online? Use the share button to save gift ideas directly to Gift Memo. No app switching, no trying to remember later.

Get Reminded Before It's Too Late

Set birthday reminders for the people you care about. Choose when to be notified: two weeks before, a few days before, or the day of. When the reminder arrives, your saved gift ideas are right there waiting.

Plan Together With a Partner

Share your gift list with your spouse or partner. Coordinate who's buying what, avoid duplicates, and make holiday planning easier.

Everything in One Place

  • Add people and their birthdays
  • Create custom events (anniversaries, holidays, etc.)
  • Save gift ideas with photos, prices, and links
  • Mark gifts as purchased
  • Set budgets per person or event

Syncs Across All Your Devices

Everything syncs automatically through iCloud. Save a gift idea on your iPhone, see it instantly on your iPad.

Who It's For

Parents tracking extended family birthdays. Partners coordinating holiday shopping. Anyone who spots great gifts months early but forgets them when the occasion arrives.

No complicated features. No social pressure. Just a simple tool to help you remember the people you care about—and the gifts they'd love.

Download Gift Memo and never forget the perfect gift again.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Spade Studio Ltd ("we", "us", or "our") built Gift Memo as a personal productivity app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use Gift Memo.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • People: Names and birthdays of people you add to the app
  • Gift Ideas: Gift names, prices, URLs, and photos you save
  • Events: Event names and dates you create
  • Photos: Images you capture or select from your photo library

Information from Device Features

With your permission, Gift Memo may access:

  • Contacts: To import names and birthdays from your device contacts
  • Camera: To take photos of gift ideas
  • Photo Library: To select existing photos

You can deny these permissions and still use the app with manual data entry.

Automatically Collected Information

We do not automatically collect any information.

How We Store Your Data

Local Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data is encrypted by iOS.

Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services. Gift Memo does not collect telemetry or usage data.

Network Requests

When you share a product URL to Gift Memo, the app fetches publicly available product information (title, price, image) directly from that website to help you save gift ideas.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties. Your data stays on your device.

Data Retention

Your data remains on your device until you delete it. We do not have access to your data.

Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • View: All your data is visible within the app
  • Edit: You can modify any information at any time
  • Delete: You can delete individual items or use "Erase All Data" in Settings to permanently delete all data from your device

Children's Privacy

Gift Memo does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences to manage gift-giving.

Security

Your data is protected by:

  • iOS device encryption
  • Your device passcode and/or biometric authentication

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Apple App Store

Gift Memo is distributed through the Apple App Store and complies with Apple's App Store Guidelines and privacy requirements.

This privacy policy applies to the Gift Memo app available on the Apple App Store.
